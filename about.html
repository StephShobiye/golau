About

Practical clarity, delivered calmly

Golau is Welsh for light. Golau Insight exists to bring clarity and structure to complex education situations, particularly where neurodivergence and transition planning are involved.

Approach

  • Structured sessions with a clear outcome
  • Focus on decisions, priorities, and next steps
  • Plain language, evidence, and practical wording
  • Boundaried support to prevent overload

Professional context

Work includes supporting neurodivergent learners and families to understand systems, plan transitions, and request appropriate adjustments.
