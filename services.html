University Transition and DSA Strategy Intensive

A structured session for neurodivergent students and families preparing for higher education. We map support needs, adjustment requests, and next steps.

Includes

  • DSA readiness roadmap and timeline
  • Adjustment and accommodation strategy
  • What to document and how to organise it
  • Email templates and wording where helpful
  • Written summary of next steps

Typical format: 90 to 120 minutes remote session + written summary.

Strategic Case Mapping Session

A calm, structured mapping session for complex education situations. We focus on priorities, leverage, and a realistic action sequence.

Best for people who are overwhelmed and need clarity and structure, not more information.

Schools and professionals

External strategic input for learning support and transition planning. Suitable for sixth forms and schools supporting neurodivergent students into post-16 and HE pathways.

If you are a school, contact us for availability and fee structure.

Scope and boundaries

Services are strategic advisory. We do not provide therapy, crisis counselling, or legal representation. If emotional distress is central, therapeutic support is recommended.
