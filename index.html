Education systems advisory

Clear strategy for complex education situations

Golau Insight Ltd provides structured, practical advisory sessions for neurodivergent students and families. Our focus is calm clarity, evidence-based next steps, and a plan you can implement.

View services Enquire about availability

Note: This is strategic advisory, not therapy, crisis counselling, or legal representation.

What you get

  • A clear goal for the session and a realistic action sequence
  • Simple frameworks to reduce overwhelm and improve decision making
  • Draft wording for emails and requests where helpful
  • A written summary of agreed next steps

Who this is for

  • Students preparing for university or post-16 transitions
  • Families navigating disability support and adjustments
  • People who want structure, not endless information
  • Schools and professionals seeking an external strategic perspective

Calm and boundaried

Sessions stay focused on strategy and decisions. Emotional support is important, and best provided by therapeutic services.

Practical deliverables

Each session is designed to produce a plan. You leave with clarity, priorities, and wording you can use.

Remote delivery

Most work is delivered remotely via video call, which makes it accessible and flexible.
