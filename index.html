Education strategy advisory

Clear strategy for complex education situations

Golau Insight Ltd provides focused, strategic advisory sessions for neurodivergent students, families and education professionals. The aim is clarity, structured decision-making, and a practical action plan you can implement immediately.

View services Enquire about availability

Strategic advisory only. Not therapy, crisis counselling, or legal representation.

What you receive

  • A clearly defined objective for the session
  • A structured action sequence with priorities
  • Frameworks to reduce overwhelm and support decision-making
  • Suggested wording for key communications where appropriate
  • A concise written summary of agreed next steps

Who this is for

  • Students preparing for university or post-16 transitions
  • Families navigating disability support and adjustments
  • Individuals seeking clarity rather than excessive information
  • Schools or professionals seeking external strategic perspective

Calm and boundaried

Sessions remain focused on strategy and decisions. Emotional support is important and best provided by appropriate therapeutic services.

Practical outcomes

Each session is designed to produce a tangible plan with clear priorities and language you can confidently use.

Remote delivery

Advisory work is delivered remotely via video consultation, providing accessibility and flexibility.
